Human People is a musical improv theater comedy troupe from Austin, Texas, y'all.

Greg founded Human People in 2016 from a musical improv jam at his cat-filled apartment, and here we are now, playing in shows across Austin, San Antonio, Houston, and New York City. We like guitar-backed big numbers, crazy characters, and rocking the stage. Here are some of our players:
